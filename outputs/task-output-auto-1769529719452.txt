**Understanding the Requirements:**

Before creating a meal plan, it's crucial to consider the following factors:

3. **Meal frequency and timing:** Eating smaller, frequent meals can help regulate hunger and metabolism.
4. **Food preferences and allergies:** It's essential to consider individual food preferences and allergies when creating a meal plan.
