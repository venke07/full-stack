# Weekly Meal Plan for an Obese Person

## General Guidelines:
- **Caloric Intake**: Aim for a daily caloric deficit; consult with a healthcare provider for personalized caloric needs.
- **Hydration**: Drink plenty of water throughout the day (aim for at least 8 cups).
- **Physical Activity**: Incorporate regular physical activity as advised by a healthcare provider.

---

## Day 1:
**Breakfast**:  
- Oatmeal (1/2 cup cooked) topped with berries (1/2 cup) and a sprinkle of cinnamon.  
- Green tea or black coffee (no sugar).

**Snack**:  
- 1 medium apple.

**Lunch**:  
- Grilled chicken salad (4 oz chicken breast, mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and balsamic vinaigrette).  
- 1 whole grain roll.

**Snack**:  
- Carrot sticks (1 cup) with hummus (2 tbsp).

**Dinner**:  
- Baked salmon (4 oz) with steamed broccoli (1 cup) and quinoa (1/2 cup cooked).

---

## Day 2:
**Breakfast**:  
- Greek yogurt (1 cup) with honey (1 tsp) and sliced almonds (1 tbsp).

**Snack**:  
- 1 banana.

**Lunch**:  
- Turkey and avocado wrap (whole grain tortilla, 4 oz turkey, 1/4 avocado, lettuce, and tomato).  
- Side of mixed fruit (1 cup).

**Snack**:  
- Celery sticks (1 cup) with peanut butter (1 tbsp).

**Dinner**:  
- Stir-fried tofu (4 oz) with mixed vegetables (1 cup) and brown rice (1/2 cup).

---

## Day 3:
**Breakfast**:  
- Smoothie (1 cup spinach, 1/2 banana, 1/2 cup almond milk, and 1 scoop protein powder).

**Snack**:  
- 1 orange.

**Lunch**:  
- Lentil soup (1 cup) with a side salad (mixed greens, cucumber, and vinaigrette).

**Snack**:  
- 1/4 cup mixed nuts (unsalted).

**Dinner**:  
- Grilled shrimp (4 oz) with zucchini noodles (1 cup) and marinara sauce (1/2 cup).

---

## Day 4:
**Breakfast**:  
- Scrambled eggs (2) with spinach and tomatoes.

**Snack**:  
- 1 pear.

**Lunch**:  
- Quinoa salad (1/2 cup quinoa, black beans, corn, diced peppers, and lime dressing).

**Snack**:  
- Greek yogurt (1/2 cup) with berries.

**Dinner**:  
- Baked chicken thighs (4 oz) with roasted Brussels sprouts (1 cup) and sweet potato (1 small).

---

## Day 5:
**Breakfast**:  
- Whole grain toast (1 slice) with avocado (1/2) and poached egg (1).

**Snack**:  
- 1 cup of mixed berries.

**Lunch**:  
- Tuna salad (4 oz tuna, mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and olive oil dressing).

**Snack**:  
- 1 small cucumber with tzatziki (2 tbsp).

**Dinner**:  
- Beef stir-fry (4 oz lean beef, bell peppers, and broccoli) with cauliflower rice (1 cup).

---

## Day 6:
**Breakfast**:  
- Chia pudding (1/4 cup chia seeds soaked in 1 cup almond milk overnight) topped with fruit.

**Snack**:  
- 1 kiwi.

**Lunch**:  
- Grilled vegetable sandwich (whole grain bread, grilled zucchini, eggplant, and hummus).

**Snack**:  
- 1 small handful of walnuts.

**Dinner**:  
- Baked cod (4 oz) with asparagus (1 cup) and a side of wild rice (1/2 cup).

---

## Day 7:
**Breakfast**:  
- Smoothie bowl (1 cup spinach, 1/2 banana, 1/2 cup almond milk, topped with sliced fruit and seeds).

**Snack**:  
- 1 apple with almond butter (1 tbsp).

**Lunch**:  
- Chickpea salad (1 cup chickpeas, diced cucumber, tomatoes, parsley, and lemon dressing).

**Snack**:  
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese with pineapple.

**Dinner**:  
- Grilled turkey burger (4 oz) on a lettuce wrap with tomato and onion, served with sweet potato fries (baked).

---

## Notes:
- Adjust portion sizes based on individual caloric needs.
- Consult with a healthcare provider or a registered dietitian before starting any new meal plan, especially for weight loss.
- Incorporate a variety of foods to ensure a balanced intake of nutrients.
